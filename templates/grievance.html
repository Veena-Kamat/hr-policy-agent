Raise a Grievance
All proceedings are strictly confidential — raising a concern in good faith is fully protected
🔒  Retaliation against anyone who raises a grievance is itself a disciplinary matter at Novaris Solutions.
Formal Grievance Process
6 steps
1
Email HR in writing
Send your concern to hr@novarissolutions.ae — include the nature of the issue, names of individuals involved, and the outcome you are seeking.
2
Acknowledgement within 3 working days
HR confirms receipt and explains next steps in writing.
3
Investigation (up to 15 working days)
An independent investigating manager interviews relevant parties and reviews documentation.
4
Grievance hearing
You are invited to a formal hearing. You have the right to bring a colleague as a companion.
5
Written outcome within 5 working days
HR communicates the decision, findings, any actions to be taken, and your right to appeal.
↑
Appeal (if needed)
Submit a written appeal to the HR Director within 5 working days. A senior manager not involved in the original case hears the appeal. The decision is final.
