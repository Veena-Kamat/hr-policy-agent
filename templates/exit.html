Resignation Process
How to resign
Submit written notice to your line manager and HR at hr@novarissolutions.ae
Notice period start
Begins the day after written notice is received by HR
Annual leave during notice
Annual leave may not ordinarily be taken during the notice period unless agreed in writing by HR
Payment in lieu (PILON)
The Company may, at its discretion, make a payment in lieu rather than require you to work out the notice period
Last working day
HR will confirm your last working day and initiate the exit process including IT access, equipment return, and final settlement
Final Settlement
Within 14 days of last working day
✓  Under UAE Labour Law, all final entitlements must be paid within 14 calendar days of your last working day.
Outstanding salary
All days worked up to and including your last working day
Accrued annual leave
Any untaken leave from the current leave year, paid at your daily salary rate
Air ticket allowance
Prorated for the months served in the current year if not yet paid — subject to eligibility (1 year service)
End of Service Gratuity
Calculated on your last drawn salary — 21 days/year for first 5 years, 30 days/year beyond 5 years
Excess leave taken
If you have taken more leave than accrued, the excess will be deducted from your final payment

EOSG on Resignation
Are you entitled?
Yes — EOSG is not forfeited on resignation under UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 33 of 2021
Minimum service
You must have completed at least 1 year of continuous service
Notice requirement
You must serve your full notice period. EOSG may be affected if notice is not served.
Probation Period Exit
Notice during probation
14 calendar days written notice — applies to both employee and company
EOSG during probation
Not applicable — minimum 1 year service required
Annual leave on exit during probation
Accrued leave is paid out, but may not be taken during the probation notice period
